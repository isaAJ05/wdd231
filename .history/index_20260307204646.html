Barranquilla, Colombia

Colombia is a diverse and vibrant country in the northwestern part of South America. It is known for its rich ethnic mix of Mestizos, Afro-Colombians, Indigenous peoples, and Europeans. Colombia is one of the most biodiverse countries in the world, with ecosystems ranging from the Amazon rainforest to the Andes mountains. The culture is lively, with famous festivals like the Carnival of Barranquilla and musical genres such as Cumbia and Vallenato, which feature traditional instruments like the accordion.
